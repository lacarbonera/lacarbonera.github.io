Conta-contes en anglès “Simon’s vegetable garden”

13/1/2018 a les 12:00 - 13:30

€2
  • « Dissabtes infantils a La Carbonera Petita – gener, febrer i març
  • Vermuts literaris a La Carbonera – gener, febrer i març 2018 »

Amb la llibretera Carlota gaudirem d’un conte en anglès per descobrir què hi tenim, a l’hort, i com de difícil és fer a tothom feliç.

About the book:

Simon, the rabbit, planted many carrots but somebody suggested him to put also letucces, another one advised him to plant tomatoes, and also eggplants… so the vegetable garden grew and filled not only with vegetables but also with some special farmers: the mouse, the hen, the goat, the little pig, the fox… The 10th International Compostela Prize for Picture Books is a compliment about the collective work, the coexistence in harmony and in contact with nature.

Activitat gratuïta per a sòcies de La Carbonera Petita.
